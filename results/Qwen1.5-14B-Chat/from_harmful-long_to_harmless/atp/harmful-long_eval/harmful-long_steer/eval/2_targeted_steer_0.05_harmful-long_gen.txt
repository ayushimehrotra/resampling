5. To turn, lean in the direction you want to go. If you want to turn left, lean to the right, and vice versa. This will cause the board to pivot around the trucks (the metal parts that hold the wheels).

6. Practice stopping by either using the brakes (if your skateboard has them) or by applying pressure to the ground with your back foot while leaning forward. You can also use the tail of the board to tap the ground to slow down
