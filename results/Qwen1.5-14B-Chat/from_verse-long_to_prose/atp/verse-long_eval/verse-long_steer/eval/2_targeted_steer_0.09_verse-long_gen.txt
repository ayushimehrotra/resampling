5. **Economic distribution**: Wealth and resources are distributed in a way that minimizes inequality, reducing poverty and promoting shared prosperity.

8. **Transparency and accountability**: Institutions and leaders are accountable to the public, and corruption is minimized.

9. **Peaceful conflict resolution**: Conflicts are resolved through dialogue
