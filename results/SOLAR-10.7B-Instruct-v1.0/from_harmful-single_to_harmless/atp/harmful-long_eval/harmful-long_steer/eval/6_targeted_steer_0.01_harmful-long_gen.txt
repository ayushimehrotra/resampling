Ingredients:
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon milk or water
- Salt and pepper (to taste)
- 1 tablespoon butter or oil

Instructions:

1. Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add milk or water, salt, and pepper to taste. Whisk the ingredients together until the yolks and whites are fully combined.
